    • HRLC Data

    We are providing links to our preliminary High Resolution Land Cover data for 2013. This data covers all areas of Puget Sound for which we had bare earth Lidar data available. This data is in a partially finished stage with any land cover classes marked "Final" being considered final and good data. The modeling process starts with candidate classes and through a series of logic rules, moves candidate land cover polygons into Final polygons. Wria 7, the Snohomish, had a processing error and has not been completed. All areas will likely be repeated as party of the 2013-2015 HRCD assuming the funding for completion is obtained in the 2017-2019 state operating budget.
